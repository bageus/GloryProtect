# Stage 6.1 post assignment rules

- A defender keeps a persistent combat type independently from the temporary work post.
- Both melee defenders and unlocked shooters may operate driver, anchor, turret, and medical posts.
- Releasing a post restores the defender's previous combat type.
- A work-post target remains reserved while its defender is dead or returning after respawn.
- Repeated deaths while returning do not erase that target.
- A monster reaching the top of a rope may enter the platform even when the anchor operator stands at the exit; combat resolves the overlap after boarding.
